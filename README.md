# One-shot-game-v2

Make a game using the p5.js library and the p5.js physics engine. 

The game will be a stickman fighting game.

Create a platform/island in the middle and a smaller platform on both sides of the middle platform slightly elevated for the TWO stickmen to spawn on. There will be a void below the platforms where the stickmen can die if they fall in. The stickman who stands last will win a point. The first player to get 5 points wins and a menu screen will pop up to restart the match and only after restarting the match. The stickman who spawns on the left platform will be controlled using the controls, ‘w’ to jump, ‘a’ and ‘d’ to move left and right. The stickman on the other side will be controlled by using the up arrow key to jump and left and right arrow keys to move left and right. The movement of the stickmen will be ragdoll-like and they will be facing the direction they are currently moving in and their weapons will also be on that side of their body. The stickmen will have different colors to help distinguish which player you are.

There will be two weapons in the middle close enough for the stickmen to get at least one weapon each. The weapon will be sticks. When the stickmen gets hit by the stick of the other stickman then they will be knocked back by a lot but not enough to get pushed off into the void.

The canvas should be centered in the window.
